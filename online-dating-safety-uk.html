What Is Online Dating Safety?

Online dating safety refers to practices that help users protect themselves from scams, fake identities, and harmful interactions while using dating apps or websites.

It includes verifying profiles, avoiding personal data sharing, and using secure communication channels within apps.

The main goal is to ensure a safe and positive dating experience without risk of fraud or exploitation.

Common Online Dating Scams

Romance scams are one of the most common issues where fake users build emotional trust and later ask for money or personal information.

Catfishing is another major issue where fake profiles use stolen photos to deceive users into believing they are someone else.

Phishing links and external payment requests are also common tactics used by scammers on dating platforms.
